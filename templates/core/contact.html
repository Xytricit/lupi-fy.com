Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start writing on Lupify?

Sign up for an account, go to your dashboard, and click "Create New Post". Our editor is intuitive and supports rich text formatting.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from your profile settings. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How do communities work?

Communities are spaces where like-minded people gather to discuss topics of interest. You can create a community, join existing ones, and participate in discussions.

Is Lupify free?

Yes! Lupify is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to a platform for creative expression without financial barriers.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you encounter any inappropriate or harmful content, please use the report button on the post or contact our support team at support@lupify.com.

Can I monetize my content?

Currently, Lupify is a free platform focused on community and expression. Monetization features may be added in the future.
